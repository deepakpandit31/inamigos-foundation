Our Programs

Comprehensive initiatives designed to drive sustainable global impact.

Education Access

Project Bachpanshala

Ensuring quality education and holistic learning experiences for underprivileged children. We build schools, train local teachers, and provide essential study materials to children who otherwise would be forced into labor.

Key Objectives:

  • Establish 50 new rural classrooms by 2026.
  • Provide mid-day meals to improve attendance.
  • Integrate digital literacy via solar-powered tablets.

Relief Work

Project Seva

Providing essential food, nutrition, and clothing to the underprivileged people in need. Access to basic medical care and daily hot meals is critical for these vulnerable communities.

Key Objectives:

  • Provide daily nutritious meals to 5,000+ individuals.
  • Conduct bi-weekly health and sanitation camps.
  • Distribute seasonal clothing and blankets.

Environment

Project Prakriti

Environmental conservation, zero-waste initiatives, and sustainability efforts. We aim to protect Mother Earth through extensive tree-planting campaigns and community cleanups.

Key Objectives:

  • Plant 10,000 native trees across urban centers.
  • Promote sustainable living and zero-waste habits.
  • Clean up major waterways and local parks.

Animal Welfare

Project Jeev

Animal welfare, rescue efforts, protection, and daily feeding programs for strays. We stand up for those who cannot speak for themselves.

Key Objectives:

  • Feed 2,000 stray animals daily.
  • Provide immediate veterinary care for injured street animals.
  • Conduct awareness programs for animal compassion.
