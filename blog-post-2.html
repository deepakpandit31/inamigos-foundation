New Solar Tablets Unveiled for Rural Classrooms

David Kim Sep 28, 2024

The digital divide remains one of the largest obstacles to educational equality. Today, we are proud to announce the successful deployment of 500 rugged, solar-powered tablets to three of our most remote partner schools.

"Access to the internet is access to the collective knowledge of humanity. It should not be restricted by zip code or power grid."

Why Solar?

Many of our partner schools operate entirely off-grid. Traditional computers or standard tablets simply aren't viable when electricity access is either non-existent or highly sporadic. By partnering with a leading green-tech firm, these devices can operate for 12 hours on a single 3-hour solar charge.

The Impact on Curriculum

These tablets come pre-loaded with an offline curriculum repository containing standard textbooks, interactive math software, and over 1,000 science video modules. The results have been phenomenal, with student engagement levels reaching an all-time high within the first month.
