100,000 Trees: Reaching Our Plantation Milestone

Michael Chang Sep 15, 2024

When we launched our 'Green Canopies' initiative two years ago, planting one hundred thousand trees seemed like an incredibly lofty aspiration. Today, thanks to the sheer willpower of our volunteers, we have planted sapling number 100,000.

"A society grows great when old men plant trees whose shade they know they shall never sit in." - Greek Proverb

More Than Just Numbers

We ensure that every tree planted is a native species conducive to the local biosphere. We don't just 'plant and forget'. Our community partnerships ensure that the saplings are watered and protected through their most vulnerable infant stages. The current survival rate of our plantations stands at a proud 88%.

The Road to 1 Million

As we celebrate this milestone, we set our sights immediately on the horizon. Our next logistical scaling operation will focus on the 'Million Tree Challenge'. We invite every corporate partner, school, and local community to join us in re-greening our beautiful earth.
