Our Story

How It All Began

The Inamigos Foundation was established in 2012 by a group of passionate social workers who witnessed the glaring inequalities in rural education and healthcare.

What started as a small initiative to provide books to a single village school has now evolved into a global movement affecting millions. We realized early on that true empowerment doesn't come from charity alone, but from building sustainable pathways to self-reliance.

Today, Inamigos operates in over 15 states, employing a holistic community-development model that tackles the root causes of poverty through education, healthcare, and economic empowerment.

Our Mission

To empower vulnerable communities by providing access to quality education, primary healthcare, and skill development programs, enabling them to lead lives of dignity and purpose.

Our Vision

A world where every individual, regardless of their socio-economic background, has the opportunity and resources to fulfill their potential and contribute to society.

Our Journey

History & Milestones

2012

Foundation Established

Started operations with a small team of 5 volunteers focusing purely on local school supply distribution.

2015

First Healthcare Initative

Launched our first mobile medical clinic, reaching over 10 remote villages lacking access to basic doctors.

2019

Global Expansion

Partnered with international bodies to extend our educational programs to neighboring countries.

2024

Digital Empowerment

Initiated the 'Tech for All' campaign, establishing 50+ computer labs in underfunded rural schools.
